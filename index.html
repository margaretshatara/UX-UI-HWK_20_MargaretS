Project Scope

The Canadian Cancer Society website has been designed to help family members, friends and communities eradicate cancer and improve the quality of life of people living with Cancer.

With users dealing with the stress of being in-directly affected by cancer, how can we simplify and re-organise the website to create a smooth and easy experience?

Problem Statement

With cancer patients, family and friends dealing with exceptionally high stress, the lack of community support found on the Canadian Cancer Society's website has proven to negatively affect the users well being by causing additional anxiety due to the lack of mental support.Furthermore, the disorganised and text heavy website causes further confusion when navigating through the website which could potentially lead to frustrations and users exiting the page to use another resource.
